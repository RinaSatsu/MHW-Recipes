Meowscular Chef's Platter: Picanha Skewer

Image source


Description


Serves: 8-10

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 lb or 1-1,4 kg Picanha (rump cap)
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Cut cross-hatches across the fat cap of the beef.
  2. Slice the beef into 1.5 - 2 inch portions across the grain of the meat.
  3. Roll the beef into a C-shape and skewer.
  4. Season the beef on all sides with salt and black pepper.
  5. Grill for 10 minutes on each side or until the meat reaches your desired doneness.
