Meowscular Chef's Platter: Roast Beef

Image source


Tender sliced roast Dodohama meat will give energy to last through any hunt


Serves: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef
  • 1 egg white
  • 3 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 Tbsp rosemary
  • 1 Tbsp thyme
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Presear beef in pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 째F (230 째C).
  3. Whisk egg white into foam, add rosemary, thyme, salt and pepper. Mix together and rub into meat with garlic.
  4. Place on a roasting rack. Roast for 15 minutes, then for 1,5-2 more hours on 325 째F (160 째C).
  5. Take out of the oven and let the meat rest for 15 minutes before serving.
