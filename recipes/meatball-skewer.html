Meowscular Chef's Platter: Roast Meatball Skewer

Image source


Tasty and cheesy meatball skewers


Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb or 1 kg minced meat (beef/pork)
  • 1 oz or 32 g breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 3 oz or 80 g cheese
  • salt, pepper

Steps:

  1. Grate cheese on coarse grater.
  2. Put meat in large bowl. Add breadcrumbs, grated cheese, egg, chilli powder, salt and pepper. Mix everything together and make small bite-sized meatballs.
  3. Add oil to a non-stick pan. Fry until golden-brown on the outside.
  4. Put in oven or microwave until cooked through.
  5. Skewer 3-4 meatballs per portion and serve.
