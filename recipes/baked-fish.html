Meowscular Chef's Platter: Baked Fish with Herbs

Image source


Hot and aromatic baked Gajau


Serves: 3-4

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole fish, scaled, gutted and gills removed (like bass)
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Fresh thyme
  • Fresh sage
  • 8 tbsp (125 g) butter
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Steps:

  1. Chop herbs into small pieces.
  2. In a small saucepan melt butter. Add herbs and salt, if butter was unsalted. Mix everything together.
  3. Once butter has cooled, rub it generously into fish. It should be fully covered with the mixture. Put the rest of the butter inside.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 °F (190 °C). Put fish on a baking tray, top with slices of lemon. Bake for 40 minutes.
  5. Top with rosemary and serve.
