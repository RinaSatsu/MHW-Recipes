Meowscular Chef's Platter: Chicken Skewer

Image source


Delicious chicken skewers


Serves: 8-10

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb or 1 kg boneless skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 Tbsp cumin powder

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces and place them into a large bowl.
  2. Add oyster sauce and cumin then mix together.
  3. Skewer the chicken evenly then grill for 10-15 minutes, until cooked through.
