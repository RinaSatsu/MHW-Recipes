Meowscular Chef's Platter: Tomato and Pumpkin Soup

Image source


Hearty and creamy tomato and pumpkin soup


Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 14 oz or 400 ml canned tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Â½ cup cream
  • salt

For pumpkin puree:

  • 1 small pumpkin
  • water

Steps:

  1. In a large pot mix tomatoes, pumpkin and broth. Boil for 15 minutes.
  2. Use blender to make the soup smooth.
  3. Add cream, salt to taste and serve.

For pumpkin puree:

  1. Slice pumpkin in half. Scoop out and remove the seeds.
  2. Cut rest of the pumpkin into medium-size chunks.
  3. Put pumpkin chunks into large pot. Add water, so that it barely cover the pieces and boil
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and bring the stock to a boil for 45 minutes, until pumpkin comletely softened.
